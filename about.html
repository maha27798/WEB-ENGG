Why Tourism Matters

Tourism is a vital industry for Pakistan because it generates income, promotes cultural exchange, and improves the country's image.

Economic Benefits

  • Foreign exchange: Tourism brings in foreign currency, which can be used to import goods and services.
  • Economic growth: Tourism contributes to the country's GDP.
